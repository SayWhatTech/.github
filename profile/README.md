# SayWhatTech

Making community governance easier to understand.

---

## What is SayWhat?

SayWhat transforms complex documents into clear, searchable, and actionable answers.

Built initially for Homeowners Associations (HOAs), SayWhat helps boards and residents quickly understand governing documents, policies, and decisions—without digging through PDFs.

---

## What We Do

- 📄 Turn HOA governing documents into conversational knowledge  
- 🔍 Provide fast, accurate answers to homeowner questions  
- 🧠 Use AI to simplify legal and operational complexity  
- ⚙️ Enable self-service for boards, residents, and managers  

---

## Why It Matters

HOA documents are often:
- Hard to navigate  
- Difficult to interpret  
- Time-consuming to manage  

SayWhat makes them accessible—reducing friction, improving transparency, and saving time for everyone involved.
